01 / Getting started

Three steps. No terminal required.

STEP 01

Download & Install

Download the zip, unzip it, and double-click INSTALL.command. That's it.

STEP 02

One Click to Sort

Double-click the Sort shortcut anytime. 13 categories, fully automatic.

STEP 03

Made a Mistake? Undo

Double-click the Undo shortcut. Every file goes right back.

03 / Why it's good

Small tool. Serious manners.

  • Sorts into 13 smart categories
  • Fully customizable — edit config.json to add your own
  • One-click undo if anything goes wrong
  • Runs entirely on your Mac — nothing collected
  • Open source and free, forever
04 / Privacy

Your files never leave.

Downloads Janitor runs 100% locally on your Mac. No files are uploaded. No data is collected. No internet connection required. No account needed. Your files stay yours.
